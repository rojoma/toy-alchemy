# Concrete Examples
version: 1.0
last_updated: 2025-04-07

## Purpose
Ground abstract concepts in everyday objects and situations
familiar to a 12-15 year old.

## Age-appropriate anchors
- Fractions: pizza slices, sharing snacks
- Ratios: mixing juice concentrate
- Speed/time/distance: cycling to school, train journey
- Proportions: resizing a photo, scaling a recipe
- Area: bedroom floor, school yard

## Technique
1. Introduce the real-world object first
2. Ask student to describe it in their own terms
3. Map the description onto the abstract concept
4. Return to the abstract form after understanding is confirmed

## Caution
Avoid examples that assume economic privilege.
Use universally accessible contexts.
