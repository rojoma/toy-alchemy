How is it different?

Any AI chatbot can spit out the answer. We're trying to build a tutor that meets the student where they are — in how they think, not just what they know.

🧭
Four teacher personalities

Warm Coach, Prof. Tanaka, Ms. Rivera, Cool Mentor. Pick the one whose warmth, pace, and style matches your child.

📝
Tell it what to work on

"Textbook p.42 word problems", "just dividing fractions" — the student can describe the exact scope they want help with.

📊
Progress you can see

Per-topic proficiency, streak days, learning gains — all visible on a simple dashboard that helps parents watch without hovering.

👀
A second AI checks the first

Our Principal Agent watches every turn and scores the teacher on six pedagogy-grounded signals. No teacher moment goes unexamined.

💬
The student rates the teacher

After each session students tell us how the teacher felt. That signal feeds back into how the AI teaches next time.

FAQ

Is it free?
Yes, free right now. If that changes we'll announce in advance.
What ages?
Aimed at elementary through high school, with the most testing on 6th-grade math. Students under 18 should use with a parent's consent — see our Terms of Service.
What happens to what my child types?
It's kept so we can show progress and history. We don't sell or share it. You can request disclosure or deletion anytime. See our Privacy Policy.
Can the AI be wrong?
Yes. The Principal Agent catches many errors but not all. Treat the tutor as a helpful study partner — confirm important work with a teacher or parent.
Does it replace school?
No — it's a supplement. Use it alongside school, not instead of it.
